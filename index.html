Incorporated in the year 2009 at Kochi, Kerala, we “Vijaya Traders” are a Sole Proprietorship (Individual) based company, engaged as the trader of Plinth Wood Block, Plinth Leg, Plinth Cover, Edge Band Tape and many more.
